Glass

♻ Recyclable

  • Jars for sauces, condiments, food products, medicines, perfumes, cleaning or beauty products.
  • Glass cups, jars, bottles.
  • Colored or transparent glass.
  • Shards of any of the above products.


    • ❌ Non-recyclable

  • Glass that has undergone a chemical process - mirrors, window glass, car glass, tempered glass, Pyrex.
  • Light bulbs, television tubes and valves.
  • Medicine vials.
  • Crystal, porcelain and ceramics.
