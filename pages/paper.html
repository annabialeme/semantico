Paper

♻ Recyclable

  • Notebooks, general office paper, photocopies, cards.
  • Newspapers, magazines, correspondence, envelopes.
  • Wrapping paper in general, tissue paper, paper bags, long-life packaging.
  • Cardboard, paperboard, egg cartons.
  • Kraft paper, heliographic paper, filter paper, drawing paper.
  • Ungreased paper towels.


    • ❌ Non-recyclable

  • Tracing paper, cellophane, carbon paper, fax paper.
  • Waxed paper or paper impregnated with waterproof substances, photographs.
  • Papers coated with some kind of paraffin, silicone or plasticized.
  • Sanitary papers or used napkins.
  • Papers that are dirty, greasy or contaminated with any substance harmful to health.
  • Adhesive tapes and labels.
  • Metallized paper or packaging.
